ZIP layout: "Download all (ZIP)" groups every asset into a folder named after each platform (e.g. youtube/, tiktok/) so you can open one folder and upload its whole kit. Select a platform tab first, then "Select all visible" + "Download selected" to grab just that network.

Videos: If your browser supports MP4 recording (Safari does, most Chromium does not) you'll get .mp4 files. Otherwise files are exported as .webm, which uploads fine to YouTube, X, Instagram and TikTok and they re-encode it server side. To force MP4 everywhere, you can re-encode the .webm with ffmpeg -i in.webm -c:v libx264 out.mp4.
