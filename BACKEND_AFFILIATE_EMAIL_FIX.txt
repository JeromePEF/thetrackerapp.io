BACKEND — Affiliate Signup: Remove Email Verification Gate
============================================================
Date: 2026-06-17

POST /api/affiliate/signup returns:
  { ok: false, error: "Email verification required before affiliate signup." }

This check blocks users from becoming affiliates until they verify
their email. The frontend already allows submission without email
verification, but the backend rejects it.

FIX:
  Remove the email verification check from the /api/affiliate/signup
  handler. Emails are verified through iMessage/SMS login codes
  inherently — the user already proved they control their phone/device.
  An extra email verification step is unnecessary friction.
