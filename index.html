        iMessage Calorie Tracker

        The Tracker App is an AI calorie tracker that lives inside iMessage. Text what you ate — "eggs and toast for breakfast" or "grilled chicken salad" — and the AI instantly calculates calories, protein, carbs, and fats. It replies with your daily totals, macro splits, and reminders to stay on track. You can also snap a photo of your meal via iMessage and the AI estimates calories from the image. No app download required — works directly inside iMessage on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Also available via Telegram. Syncs with Apple Health, Fitbit, Garmin, Whoop, Oura, and Apple Watch. Free calorie tracking with leaderboards, 7-day history, and hydration monitoring. Pro plans unlock unlimited history, body measurements, advanced analytics, AI meal planning, and photo-based logging.

        Text-Based Calorie Tracking

        Track calories by texting an AI assistant. Type your meal in natural language — the AI processes it and calculates calories and macros instantly. No barcode scanning needed. Just describe what you ate and get nutritional breakdowns back in seconds. Works as a simple calorie counter that integrates directly into your iMessage chat thread.
