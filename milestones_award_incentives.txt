🚀 How to Generate 10,000+ Milestones
To get to 10,000 without listing them all, you use a Matrix System.

Step 1: Choose the Exercise Category (e.g., Pushups, Running, Squats, Planks, Burpees, Lunges, Jump Rope, Dips, Sit-ups, Kettlebell Swings). Count: ~20 Exercises

Step 2: Choose the Metric (Reps, Miles, Minutes, Weight, Days). Count: ~5 Metrics

Step 3: Choose the Scale (The specific number).

Small: 10, 25, 50, 75, 100
Medium: 200, 500, 1,000
Large: 5,000, 10,000, 50,000, 100,000
Massive: 1 Million (Steps), 1 Billion (Reps - joking, but you get the idea). Count: ~10 Scales per metric
Step 4: Choose the Timeframe (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, All-Time). Count: 4 Timeframes

Step 5: Choose the "Club" Name (The Rookie, The Veteran, The Elite, The Legend, The Mythic). Count: 5 Titles

The Math: 20 Exercises × 5 Metrics × 10 Scales × 4 Timeframes × 5 Titles = 20,000 Unique Combinations.
